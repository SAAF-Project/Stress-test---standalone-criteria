## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes requirements for the responsible use of AI tools, including large language models (LLMs) and AI audit agents, within [Organization Name]'s audit and assurance activities.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who use AI tools in the context of internal audit, risk management, or compliance activities at [Organization Name].

Tools not listed above require prior written approval from [IT Security / CISO / Audit Leadership].

## 4. Data Classification

| Data classification | May be shared with AI tools? | Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| Public | Yes | No restrictions |
| Internal | Yes, with approved tools | Do not include personal data |
| Confidential | No | Must not be entered into any AI tool |
| Secret / Restricted | No | Must not be entered into any AI tool |

When in doubt, treat data as one level more sensitive than you believe it to be.

## 5. Acceptable Use

AI tools may be used to:
- Draft audit plans, memos, and reports (subject to human review before finalization).
- Analyze anonymized or synthetic datasets for testing and development purposes.
- Summarize public regulatory guidance and standards.
- Generate example audit procedures and control test scripts.

## 6. Unacceptable Use

AI tools must not be used to:
- Process personal data without a valid legal basis under GDPR.
- Make final audit conclusions without human review and sign-off.
- Access systems or data without prior authorization.
- Generate, store, or transmit credentials, passwords, or cryptographic keys.

## 7. Human Oversight

All AI-generated content used in audit documentation must be:
1. Reviewed by a qualified auditor before inclusion in working papers.
2. Clearly attributed as AI-assisted (e.g. "Drafted with AI assistance, reviewed by [Name]").
3. Validated against source evidence where factual claims are made.

## 8. Incident Reporting

Report any suspected misuse of AI tools, unexpected AI behavior, or data exposure to [Security contact / CISO] within [X] hours of discovery.

## 9. Training

All users of approved AI tools must complete [AI Awareness Training] before use and annually thereafter.

## 10. Review

This policy will be reviewed annually by [Owner] and updated to reflect changes in approved tools, regulatory requirements, or organizational risk appetite.
